panacea /pænə'siə/
thuốc bách bệnh, an ancient Greek goddess of healing.

NOUN

  • a remedy for all disease or ills; cure-all.
  • an answer or solution for all problems or difficulties His economic philosophy is a good one, but he tries to use it as a panacea.

SYNONYMS FOR PANACEA

  1. elixir
  2. nostrum

elixir /i'liksə/
(dược học) cồn ngọt, thuốc luyện đan, thuốc tiên elixir of life: thuốc trường sinh bất lão

NOUN

  • the quintessence or absolute embodiment of anything.
  • a panacea; cure-all; sovereign remedy.

SYNONYMS

  1. philosopher's stone
